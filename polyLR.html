❀ Ｓｃｅｎａｒｉｏ：

As a data scientist in HR department, you are given a dataset containing info of 10 employees in your company, with each employee's position level and current salary. By analyzing this data, you are asked to find the relationship between an employee's position level and salary. By doing so, you must also construct an algorithm to estimate a salary to be offered to a new hire according to her/his assigned position level in the company.


❀ Ａｐｐｒｏａｃｈ：　

 ✣ *Polynomial of degree 4 was chosen.

 ✣ 1. For data preprocessing, the x column (Position Level),[x1,...,x10] was transformed into n exponential columns of [x1, ...,x10], [x12,...,x102], [x13,...,x103], [x14,...,x104].

 ✣ 2. With the processed data, Multiple Linear Regression was performed with the n exponential x terms and Salary for all 10 data points (Splitting the data into train & test set was not performed, since there were only 10 datapoints).

 ✣ Independent Variable(x): Position Level
 ✣ Dependent Variable(y): Salary


❀ Ｐｙｔｈｏｎ　Ｃｌａｓｓｅｓ　Ｕｓｅｄ：

 ✣ (from sklearn.preprocessing) PolynomialFeatures
 ✣ (from sklearn.linear_model) LinearRegression
